Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie

Description

A cold refreshing smoothie for a warm summer day. Containing blackberry and raspberry, the smoothie is good for your health.

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • .5 cup of blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 (6 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes
  1. Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes into a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!
